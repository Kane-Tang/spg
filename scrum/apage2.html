Sprint planning

Sprint planning is a meeting that is held between the Scrum Master, Product Owner and the rest of the Agile team. It is usually timeboxed to an hour and the team members meet to discuss and create a sprint backlog – decide what tasks from the backlog should be worked on in a certain sprint. The acceptance criteria are also discussed, and effort needed to perform the work is estimated. The top priority tasks are usually planned first and if there is room for lesser priority tasks, they are added after. What the team takes on is dependent on the length of the sprint, the team’s capacity, and velocity.
