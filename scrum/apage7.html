Final step

The final step of every sprint is the stage of product estimation. The product is evaluated in accordance with the team's definition of "Done" and provided to the customer to obtain his feedback. Then a new sprint starts, and the cycle repeats.
You can add more items to the product backlog if your team find it necessary. If there are items in the product backlog that are not yet been implememnted, then more sprints maybe needed; otherwise you may end the whole scrum lifecycle.
